# Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) between Lenovo ThinkPad T14 Gen 5 vs HP OmniBook 5

Objective

The purpose of this comparison is to evaluate the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) of two business laptops over a 5-year useful life.
TCO includes the purchase cost, operating expenses, maintenance, and residual value to determine which laptop provides better long-term value.

Assumptions

Useful life: 5 years

Daily usage: 8 hours

Electricity cost: SGD 0.30/kWh

One battery replacement during ownership (if required)

One minor repair outside warranty

Residual value after 5 years estimated based on resale value

Amortization

A laptop is a capital asset, so its purchase cost can be amortized (or depreciated, in accounting terms, if referring to tangible assets) over its useful life.
